About Ember

Artist, Game Designer, Creator, and Storyteller

My Story

I didn’t start out naturally good at art. In fact, for a long time, I struggled with confidence—my lines felt wrong, my ideas didn’t look how I imagined them, and I often compared myself to others. But even when it was frustrating, I kept going. Something about creating gave me comfort. Art slowly became my space to breathe, experiment, and figure things out.

As I improved, something changed—not just in my work, but in how I saw myself. Art helped me grow more confident, not only in my abilities but in my identity. It became a way for me to explore who I am and to express things I didn’t always know how to say out loud. It also helped me connect with people—sharing my art opened doors to new friendships, meaningful conversations, and a sense of belonging I hadn’t felt before

Thanks for taking the time to experience my work. I’m still learning, still evolving—but I’m proud of how far I’ve come, and excited for what’s next.
